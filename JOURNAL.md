## February 28 2026
Log 1:
I watched a few more DIY 3D printer build playlists on youtube and i decided on my mode of transmission for each axes! The y axis will be belt driven with two linear rods. The z axis will be using a lead screw but with aluminium extrusions and v wheels! (Mainly because the z axis has to fold and having the frame be the transmission makes it easier to figure out the folding part of it). The x axis is kinda where I'm uncertain, it will either be linear rails or aluminium extrusions with v wheels. I will ask around online about my choices and might change them in the future
Time spent: 2.5 hours
